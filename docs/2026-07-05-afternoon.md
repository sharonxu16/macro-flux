**Fact**

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]

> * **Transmission / Market Read**: Risk‑premium channel via higher shipping costs; supports **long Brent on spikes** but does not reverse the glut‑driven downtrend.
> * **Watchpoint / Confidence**: Whether Iran publishes a fee schedule or insurers raise war‑risk premiums in response; Confidence: Medium (single source, no enforcement detail yet).
