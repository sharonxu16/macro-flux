> The US-Iran peace deal accelerated dramatically intraday as Pakistan confirmed the final text would be signed Sunday and President Trump pivoted from denouncing terms to declaring he is “ready to end the war,” sending Brent plunging to its lowest since March below `$86/bbl`. The risk-on rotation lifted equities and dragged on gold, with investors aggressively pricing out the Hormuz supply-disruption premium in real time. The cross-market thread is a massive peace-premium unwind in crude that is compressing energy-linked inflation expectations, just as the ECB’s Nagel warns prices will stay elevated regardless and the Fed prepares to hold rates next week.
