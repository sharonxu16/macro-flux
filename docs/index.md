> [!abstract] Macro Overview
> The global macro regime is fragmenting along energy lines. **Oil** at `$126/bbl` highs is simultaneously forcing **ECB** and **BoE** toward rate hikes while the **Fed** under Powell signals cuts — a G-7 policy divergence that will define Q3 FX and rates. Beijing escalates sanctions defiance, telling Chinese firms to ignore US oil sanctions even as Trump threatens `25%` EU auto tariffs and withdraws `5,000` troops from Germany. The common thread: the **Strait of Hormuz** blockade is tightening monetary conditions globally, breaking the G-7 policy consensus, and creating asymmetric winners (Saudi, Libya, US shale) versus losers (energy-importing Asia, European industry, budget airlines).
