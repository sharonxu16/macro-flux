The RBI should temper its currency intervention efforts, a former governor said, about a month after the monetary authority imposed sweeping curbs to support a struggling rupee ([BBG](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-11/india-must-stay-course-on-opening-markets-former-rbi-head-says)).
