> [!abstract] Overview
> The afternoon session was dominated by a sharp reversal in de-escalation momentum after the White House flatly denied Iranian state media reports of a draft memorandum of understanding to reopen the Strait of Hormuz, crushing an intraday oil sell-off and erasing early European equity gains, while the ECB simultaneously warned in its Financial Stability Review that geopolitical shocks risk a sudden, sharp market repricing. The cross-market thread is a tug-of-war between geopolitical whiplash eroding risk appetite in broader equities and energy markets, while AI-driven structural demand continues to carve out its own insulated rally.
