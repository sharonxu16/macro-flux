### 📌 China's Export Strength Seen as Leverage Ahead of Xi-Trump Summit

**Fact**

Ro Khanna (D-CA) argued that the US needs a tougher, more strategic approach to China, and that lowering the cost of fertilizer and reopening the Strait of Hormuz are critical for the US economy ([BBG](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/videos/2026-05-09/ro-khanna-on-why-the-iran-conflict-is-a-gift-to-beijing-video)).
