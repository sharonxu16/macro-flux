**Fact**

Minefields and stalled talks keep the Strait of Hormuz in strategic limbo, and Iran’s grip on maritime traffic through the strait remains both “real” and “strong” even amid the cessation in hostilities ([SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/week-asia/politics/article/3359407/minefields-stalled-talks-keep-strait-hormuz-strategic-limbo)).
