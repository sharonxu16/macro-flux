> [!abstract] Overview
> A suspected Iranian drone strike on a Singapore-flagged cargo ship near the Strait of Hormuz has forced the UN to suspend its evacuation program, abruptly halting the post-war oil supply normalization narrative and injecting a fresh geopolitical risk premium. The attack compounds an already hawkish macro backdrop after May US core PCE printed at `3.4%`, the fastest since October 2023, keeping Fed rate hikes on the table even as officials point to tentative disinflation beneath the surface. Meanwhile, Micron’s blowout AI forecast ignited a global semiconductor rally, but Apple’s `6%` slide after hiking Mac and iPad prices on memory shortages underscores the cost-push tension now gripping the tech supply chain. Asian equities opened lower and the dollar held firm, with the return of Hormuz kinetic risk threatening to unwind the easing of energy-linked inflation that markets had begun pricing.

## 🔬 Narrative Watch

### 📌 Hormuz Cargo Strike Suspends UN Evacuation, Reverses Supply Normalization

**Fact**
A Singapore-flagged cargo vessel, identified by four sources as the Ever Lovely, was hit on the starboard side by an unknown projectile `7.5` nautical miles southeast of Oman’s coast, with no casualties reported ([SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/news/world/middle-east/article/3358398/singapore-flagged-cargo-ship-hit-unknown-projectile-while-crossing-hormuz)). The UN International Maritime Organization immediately paused its program to shepherd ships and seafarers through the Strait of Hormuz after the suspected attack ([SCMP 1](https://www.scmp.com/news/world/middle-east/article/3358398/singapore-flagged-cargo-ship-hit-unknown-projectile-while-crossing-hormuz), [CNN](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMiswFBVV95cUxNa0V2T3lqclJWdFBicm1RTkhieXZaTGNZS2lRRWp0SWRuMzZ4cTlJN292SmNIaFpVb3pyMGFieXdiR2lEdC1nSm5IYTZlOWhJYnB2OU05X0lxdjJ0dTJjMXo3bjFjZFJyTWVkelhybXl4RE9XUEx6ODdoRGc3ekZNX0FSbkdsM1EwUEp6NWdLd2JNQzFNT1F2TUNWMndGSTl1RkFwOEhyOEYwZEFRZENyc05nYw)). Two US officials said Iran was behind the attack; a security source said the projectile was likely a drone ([SCMP 2](https://www.scmp.com/news/world/middle-east/article/3358398/singapore-flagged-cargo-ship-hit-unknown-projectile-while-crossing-hormuz)). International oil prices rose on June 25 amid the heightened regional tensions ([Xinhua_Fortune](https://www.news.cn/fortune/20260626/1f728c90fe4a424290ba044e160541a3/c.html)).

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]
