### 📌 Bank Indonesia deploys a shock half‑point hike as Prabowo seizes export controls

**Fact**
Indonesia’s central bank delivered a larger‑than‑expected interest‑rate increase to intensify its defense of the rupiah after the currency tumbled to successive record lows this month ([BBG 1](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-20/bank-indonesia-surprises-markets-with-half-point-rate-hike)). Indonesian President Prabowo Subianto said his government will take control of the country’s commodities exports, a sweeping and sudden assertion of power that evokes the country’s past authoritarianism and state grip on the economy ([BBG 2](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-20/prabowo-to-tighten-controls-of-indonesia-s-commodity-exports)).
