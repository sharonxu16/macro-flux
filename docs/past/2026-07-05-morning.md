## 🔬 Narrative Watch

### 📌 Oil Supply Wave Sparks Glut Fears as Hormuz Normalizes

**Fact**
Oil prices are falling everywhere as a peace deal between the US and Iran unleashes a wave of supply, overwhelming demand from buyers and prompting talk of a glut of crude ([BBG 1](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-04/oil-s-stunning-reversal-rekindles-fears-of-a-global-glut)). Tankers are making unusual U-turns in and around the Strait of Hormuz, with some vessels opting to take a route through Iranian waters instead of the standard transit corridor, according to ship tracking data ([BBG 2](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-04/tankers-u-turn-in-hormuz-with-some-taking-iran-route-instead)).

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]
> * **Narrative change**: Acceleration of Hormuz de-escalation, with physical flows normalizing faster than expected and swelling global crude balances.
> * **Transmission / Market Read**: Supply channel tilts risks toward **lower Brent** and **shorter energy-linked equities**, while easing inflation pressures broadly.
> * **Watchpoint / Confidence**: First week of post-deal tanker loadings from Iranian terminals; if volumes exceed `1.5 mbd` without incident, the glut narrative hardens. Confidence: High due to cross-source vessel-tracking data.

### 📌 China Rotates Coast Guard Patrol East of Taiwan

**Fact**
Beijing on Saturday rotated a new coastguard task force into waters east of Taiwan. The Xiushan ship formation has replaced the Daishan group to continue ‘routine law enforcement patrols’, Beijing said, as Taiwan shadowed the fleet ([SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/news/china/diplomacy/article/3359426/beijing-sends-new-coastguard-force-waters-east-taiwan)).

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]
> * **Narrative change**: Confirmation of sustained patrol operations without escalation; the rotation is procedural, not an expansion.
> * **Transmission / Market Read**: Low-level risk premium persists on **TWD**, but no catalyst for a breakout; steady state tilts toward range-bound **USD/TWD**.
> * **Watchpoint / Confidence**: Any coastguard-vessel interaction with Taiwanese forces or new Chinese naval maneuvers; Confidence: Medium, as rotation was telegraphed and drew no immediate protest.
