> [!abstract] Overview
> **Project Freedom** kicked off Monday with a false start — a reported missile strike on a US warship in the Strait of Hormuz, quickly denied by Washington, while Iran's Revolutionary Guards unveiled a new map claiming control of the waterway. **Brent** steadied near session highs as the market repriced the probability of a direct US-Iran naval clash. Across risk assets, the AI-driven rally pushed South Korean stocks to a fresh record and Asian stocks ex-Japan to an all-time high, creating a sharp divergence between tech momentum and macro-geopolitical drag.
