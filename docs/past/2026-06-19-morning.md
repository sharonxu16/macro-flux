> [!abstract] Overview
> The Fed’s hawkish pivot accelerated as a fall in jobless claims and a rise in the Philadelphia Fed index reinforced rate‑hike bets, pushing the dollar to a fresh one‑year high and short‑dated Treasury yields to their highest since last year. **Fact**
