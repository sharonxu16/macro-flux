### 📌 China Inflation Split Widens — Factory Prices Surge, Consumer Demand Stalls

**Fact**
China’s consumer inflation unexpectedly stalled in May even as factory prices gained at the fastest pace in almost four years, storing up risks for company profits as poor demand at home seals off the economy from a global commodities rally ([BBG 1](http://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-10/chinese-consumer-inflation-unexpectedly-stalls-despite-oil-shock)). The producer price index rose by `3.9%` from a year earlier, more than the `3.5%` forecast of economists surveyed by Wind, while consumer prices rose by `1.2%`, matching estimates but remaining essentially unchanged from the prior month ([SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/plus/economy/china-economy/article/3356627/chinas-factory-price-jump-contrasts-muted-consumer-inflation)). Energy costs climbed as the Iran war cut off supply through the Strait of Hormuz ([FT](https://www.ft.com/content/6f6a385c-b9ac-47c2-a4d1-72e27bf2a8fc)).

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]
> * **Narrative change**: Acceleration of the domestic demand weakness story, now with a profitability squeeze from imported inflation; the Macro State’s “Global Demand Softening” is confirmed but with a supply‑shock twist.
> * **Transmission / Market Read**: Inflation‑mix channel weights on **Chinese equities**, particularly consumer and industrial names with thin pricing power; supports **long CNH** near‑term via higher nominal rates but medium‑term capital‑flow pressure if growth disappoints.
> * **Watchpoint / Confidence**: Next month’s CPI and profit data will show whether PPI passes through to consumer prices or margins; Confidence: High on data, Medium on market reaction given offsetting war‑premium trades.

> [!info] [AI Reasoning]
> * **Narrative change**: Acceleration — official intervention concern joins existing geopolitical risk premium on KRW, moving from verbal to enforcement threat.
> * **Transmission / Market Read**: Capital‑flow and risk‑premium channel; the inspection favours **short KRW** in the immediate term as it signals discomfort with the pace of depreciation, but may cap sharp moves if speculative positions are forced to unwind.
> * **Watchpoint / Confidence**: Any formal FX intervention or emergency stabilization statement in the next 48 h would reconfirm the defense line; Confidence: Medium — inspection alone doesn’t guarantee actual dollar selling.
