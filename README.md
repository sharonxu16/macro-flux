## Structure

- **Narrative Watch** — 3-5 most consequential macro stories. Fact → Macro Lens → Potential Market Impact.
- **Central Bank Watch** — Signals and decisions. No filler.
- **Key Global Developments** — Economic Indicators → Central Banks → Geopolitics → Equities → Commodities.
- **Cross-Market Implication** — The hidden thread connecting the day.
- **Economic Calendar** — Next 24h high-impact releases (US, CN, HK, EU, GB, JP, KR, TW, SG, AU). Consensus & prior from TradingEconomics.
- **Scenario to Watch** — 1-2 indicators/events for the next 24-48h.

## Data Sources

| Tier | Sources | Role |
|---|---|---|
| S-tier | Bloomberg, FT, WSJ, Reuters | Primary sourcing |
| A-tier | CNBC, SCMP, BBC Business | Supplementary, Asia angle |
| Sentiment | CNN (via Google News) | Mainstream sentiment gauge |
| Calendar | TradingEconomics | Consensus, prior, actual |
| Official | Fed, PBOC, ECB | Central bank press releases |

## Delivery

- **Schedule**: Overnight edition at 08:00 HKT (20:00–08:00 window)
- **Tone**: Central bank research note — measured, probabilistic, no hyperbole.
- **AI Disclaimer**: All AI-generated analysis is prefixed with `[AI Reasoning]`.
